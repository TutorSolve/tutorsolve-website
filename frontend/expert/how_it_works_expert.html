How It Works for Experts

Step 1 – Read Policies Carefully

Before applying, every expert must carefully read:

  • Expert Policy
  • Payment Policy

Experts should apply only if they fully agree with all terms and conditions.

Step 2 – Create Expert Account

To apply as an expert on TutorSolve, candidates must:

  • Register on the website
  • Create an expert account
  • Submit required details carefully

Mandatory details include:

  • Full Name
  • Email ID
  • WhatsApp Number
  • Qualification Details
  • Resume/CV
  • ID Proof (for verification purposes)
  • Subject Expertise

Experts must apply only for subjects in which they have genuine expertise and confidence.

Providing false qualifications or fake expertise may lead to permanent rejection.

Step 3 – Submit Application

After filling all required information:

  • Submit the expert application form
  • Wait for admin review and verification

Please note:

  • Submission of application does NOT guarantee approval
  • TutorSolve reviews every application manually
  • Approval depends on qualification, expertise, samples, communication quality, and platform requirements

Step 4 – Admin Review & Approval

Our team may:

  • Review your profile
  • Verify your background
  • Check samples/projects
  • Conduct evaluation tasks if required

After successful review:

  • Your account will be approved by the admin team
  • You will receive confirmation through:
  • Email
  • Website notification
  • WhatsApp (if required)

If rejected:

  • The account may remain inactive
  • Experts may reapply later with stronger samples or updated qualifications

Step 5 – Access Expert Dashboard

Once approved, experts will get access to the TutorSolve Expert Dashboard.

Inside the dashboard, experts may see:

  • Available assignments/tasks
  • Notifications
  • Deadline details
  • Task status updates
  • Communication updates
  • Payment-related updates

Step 6 – Receive Assignment Notifications

Whenever a client posts a task related to your subject expertise:

  • You may receive:
  • Dashboard notifications
  • Email alerts
  • Internal updates

Please note:

  • Receiving a notification does NOT automatically assign the task to you.

Step 7 – Review Assignment Details

Before requesting a task, experts must carefully review:

  • Subject/topic
  • Deadline
  • Word count
  • Technical requirements
  • Referencing style
  • Files/instructions
  • Complexity level

Experts should only proceed if they are fully confident they can complete the task properly.

Step 8 – Request Assignment

If interested in the assignment:

  • Submit a request for assignment allocation through the dashboard/system
  • Contact the assigned TutorSolve employee/admin only

Please note:

  • Experts are NOT allowed to contact clients directly
  • All communication must happen only through the assigned TutorSolve employee/admin
  • Direct client communication is strictly prohibited

Experts must NOT:

  • Contact clients directly
  • Contact random admins/employees unnecessarily
  • Claim tasks without confirmation

Step 9 – Discuss Task Details

Before starting work, the expert must discuss with the assigned employee/admin:

  • Deadline
  • Requirements
  • Formatting
  • Referencing style
  • Calculations/coding needs
  • Expected quality
  • Price/payment terms

Experts must clear all doubts before accepting the task.

Step 10 – Price Confirmation & Work Assignment

Once:

  • Requirements are understood
  • Price is finalized
  • Admin approves allocation

Then:

  • The task will officially be assigned
  • Expert ID/name may be linked internally with the assignment
  • Dashboard/task status may update accordingly

Only after official assignment confirmation should work begin.

Step 11 – Complete & Submit Work

Experts are expected to:

  • Submit plagiarism-free work
  • Avoid excessive AI-generated content
  • Follow university formatting guidelines
  • Meet deadlines strictly
  • Submit properly structured files

Assignments may include:

  • Reports
  • Presentations
  • Excel work
  • Coding tasks
  • Calculations
  • Case studies
  • Research work

After completing the assignment:

  • Upload/submit the final work through the dashboard/system
  • Click “Mark as Done” after successful submission

Only after marking the task as completed will the assignment move for review and payment processing.

Step 12 – Revisions & Clarifications

If revisions or explanations are required:

  • Experts must cooperate professionally
  • Minor corrections are part of normal workflow
  • Experts may need to explain work through chat or live discussion if required

Failure to cooperate may affect:

  • Payments
  • Ratings
  • Future work allocation

Step 13 – Payment Processing

After:

  • Submission
  • Quality review
  • Revision completion
  • Internal/client approval

Payment will be processed according to the Payment Policy.

Payment timelines may vary depending on:

  • Task type
  • Client clearance
  • Revision status
  • Internal review process

Important Notes

Experts must understand:

  • TutorSolve is a freelance task-based platform
  • Work availability may vary
  • Quality and consistency directly affect future opportunities
  • Poor-quality, copied, or AI-generated work is strictly prohibited
  • Professional communication is mandatory
